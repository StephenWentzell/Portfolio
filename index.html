Stephen Wentzell

A national politics reporter with ten years of experience in the field of journalism, learn more about Stephen Wentzell's career.

Stephen Wentzell is a federal politics reporter with ten years of experience in the journalism industry developing feature writing skills, editing television coverage for digital platforms and securing high-profile sources while covering multiple stories at any given time.

He is also a published author, book and film reviewer, and occasional podcaster.

Currently based in Manhattan, N.Y., he works as an Engagement Intern with the Mott Haven Herald and the Hunts Point Express. He is a Masters of Journalism student at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at the City University of New York.
